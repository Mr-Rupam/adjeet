Portfolio Section — Without Case Studies

No case study writeups available. What should the portfolio look like instead?

A

Photo Gallery (Recommended)

A filterable grid of installation photos — filter by service type and city. Each photo shows the work with a short label (service, city, year). No detail page, no writeup. Clean and honest. Works great even with just phone photos.

B

Remove Portfolio Entirely

Skip the /portfolio page. Showcase work only inside the hero and service detail pages via inline photos. Simpler, but loses a dedicated "proof" section that brand managers look for.

C

Minimal Project Cards

A grid of cards — each with a photo, service tag, city, and year. No body copy at all. Clicking opens a lightbox with the photo at full size (no separate page). A middle ground between A and B.

Note on Home Page

The "Portfolio teaser" section on Home (currently showing 6 curated case studies) will be updated to match whichever option you pick — a photo strip, removed entirely, or a mini card grid.
